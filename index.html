Baby Milk

Babies in Gaza are facing severe food shortages due to the ongoing blockade and conflict. This has led to widespread malnutrition and starvation. The lack of clean water and destruction of infrastructure further exacerbate the crisis, making it difficult for families to provide for their children. Many infants are at high risk of health complications due to inadequate nutrition and poor living conditions​

Baby Clothes

In Gaza, the plight of babies is heartbreaking. Due to the relentless blockade and ongoing conflict, there is a severe shortage of baby clothes. Families struggle to find even the most basic items to keep their infants warm and safe. Economic hardship means many parents can't afford new clothes, relying instead on sparse donations from humanitarian organizations. This leaves many babies without proper clothing, intensifying their vulnerability in an already dire situation​
